About

Ross Taylor Williams is a singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and music educator rooted in the heartland — weaving folk, indie, and soul into songs that feel like standing barefoot in wet grass.

His debut album Earth Music was born from two years of writing, wandering, and sitting with the quiet. Recorded with close collaborators in living rooms and home studios, the album channels the intimacy of the spaces where the songs first took shape.

When he's not writing or performing, Ross teaches music to the next generation of players — carrying the same patience, curiosity, and warmth into the classroom that he brings to the stage.
